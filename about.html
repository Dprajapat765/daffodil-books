About Us

Who We Are

We know what children want and we know what helps them to develop their skills and abilities as part of the exciting process of learning and growing up.
Our creative team is built-up around teachers, authors, editors, artists, designers, mums and dads.
They are always on the lookout for new development in education, presentation, technology, and communication that can contribute to keeping children's books an essential part of a child's early years.

Our Mission

We want to make cool books that are fun to use and help you learn better. We use new technology and really smart people to make learning more interesting. Our mission is to help everyone learn and enjoy it, no matter where they come from.

Our Vision

One day, all children in our country will have the opportunity to attain an excellent and affordable education. We are looking to do our share in making our nation an educational hub for the entire world.
